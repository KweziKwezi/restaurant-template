About

Welcome to Restaurant, where every dish tells a story. Founded in the heart of Gqeberha, our restaurant blends tradition with innovation to offer a dining experience like no other. From farm-fresh ingredients to recipes passed down through generations, we’re passionate about delivering authentic flavors and heartfelt service. Whether you’re joining us for a casual lunch or a special celebration, our team is here to make you feel at home, one plate at a time.

Our Journey

  • 2015 – The Beginning: The Golden Spoon opens its doors as a small, family-run eatery focused on local favorites.
  • 2017 – A Fresh Look: We expand our dining space and refresh our interior to match our growing customer base.
  • 2019 – New Menu Launch: Introduced seasonal dishes and vegetarian options to meet diverse tastes.
  • 2021 – Community Recognition: Voted “Best Local Restaurant” by Gqeberha Food Lovers’ Awards.
  • 2024 – Going Green: Launched our sustainability initiative, sourcing 80% of produce from local farms.
  • 2025 – Today: We continue to serve great food with passion and purpose, welcoming new and returning guests every day.

What we stand for

At Restaurant, we stand for more than just great food — we stand for community, culture, and care. Every meal we serve is guided by our core values: authenticity, hospitality, and sustainability. We believe in celebrating local ingredients, supporting small farmers, and honoring the traditions that inspire our menu. From the warmth of our service to the richness of our recipes, our mission is to create a space where everyone feels welcome and every dish feels like home.
